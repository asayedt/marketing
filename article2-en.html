What is Fallopian Tube Blockage?

Fallopian tube blockage refers to an obstruction in the fallopian tubes, which connect the ovaries to the uterus. When these tubes become blocked, it can affect a woman's ability to conceive.

Some of the most common symptoms of fallopian tube blockage include:

  • Lower back pain.

When Does Pregnancy Occur After Fallopian Tube Reopening?

Pregnancy after fallopian tube reopening depends on several factors, including the cause of the blockage and the overall health condition of the patient. Some women may conceive within a few months after the procedure, while others may take longer.

Regardless of these factors, medical approval is necessary before attempting to conceive.
